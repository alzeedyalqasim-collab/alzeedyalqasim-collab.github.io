About Alzeedy Lab Safety

We are dedicated to helping university students stay safe and confident during lab work.

Our Mission

CampusLab Safety was created to provide affordable, reliable, and comfortable lab safety gear for students, ensuring every lab session is both productive and secure. Our products meet standard safety requirements and are tested for durability.

Learn more about laboratory safety guidelines at OSHA Laboratory Safety .

Our Core Safety Items

Below is a short list describing some of our most important safety essentials used by students:

Lab Coat
A protective garment worn to keep clothes clean and safe from chemicals.
Safety Goggles
Used to protect your eyes from splashes, dust, and fumes during experiments.
Nitrile Gloves
Durable gloves that protect hands from harmful substances and prevent contamination.
Face Shield
Provides full-face protection from liquid splashes and chemical exposure.
